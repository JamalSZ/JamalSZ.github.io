About Me

Data Research Engineer with expertise in building high-quality datasets and scalable ML systems

I recently completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Zurich with Magna Cum Laude distinction. My research focused on large-scale data analysis, statistical modeling, and developing theoretical frameworks for multimodal AI systems.

With extensive experience in data engineering and analysis, I've built scalable data pipelines, curated high-quality datasets for ML training, and conducted comprehensive statistical analysis on real-world datasets. My work bridges academic research with practical applications in industry settings.

I have hands-on experience in collaborative research environments, having worked with industry partners at Durst Phototechnik AG where I developed anomaly detection systems that reduced equipment downtime by 23%. I excel at translating complex research insights into practical solutions for cross-functional teams.
