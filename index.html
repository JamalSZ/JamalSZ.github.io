PhD Research Impact

Model Selection & R&D Resource Allocation

Developed a predictability upper bound that provides a fundamental performance limit for time series forecasting tasks.

  • Small gap between SOTA model performance and the bound: diminishing returns on additional model complexity and training
  • Large gap: substantial potential for improvement, guiding where to invest R&D effort in model architecture and optimization

Data Quality & Process Diagnostics

Used the bound to disentangle intrinsic data noise from model inadequacy.

  • Low bounds reveal highly unpredictable processes where gains come from better data collection, feature engineering, or process redesign
  • High bounds with poor model performance point to model limitations, guiding architectural improvements and debugging
